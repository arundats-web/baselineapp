Delete your account

You can delete your Baseline account at any time. Deleting your account permanently removes all your match data, reports, AI coach insights, trends, and personal information from our servers. This action cannot be undone.

Option 1 — Recommended

Delete from within the app

The quickest way to delete your account is directly from the Baseline app.

1
Open the Baseline app on your phone
2
Tap the Profile icon on the home screen
3
Scroll down and tap Delete account
4
Confirm deletion when prompted — your account and all data will be permanently deleted immediately

⚠️ This is permanent. All your matches, reports, trends and account information will be deleted and cannot be recovered.

Option 2

Request deletion by email

If you no longer have access to the app or are unable to delete your account from within the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us. We will action your request within 30 days and confirm by email when complete.

Request account deletion

Email us from the address associated with your Baseline account and we'll delete everything within 30 days.
